Exterior Detailing

Like da outside of da car

Bring out the shine in your vehicle and knock off that road grime and bugs with an exterior detail. 3-bucket method to prevent new swirls and scratches. Pre-rinse, foam cannon, hand wash, rinse, and hand dry. Clay bar treatment is included in exterior details with customers also getting a paint correction.

Interior Detailing

Believe it or not this pretains to the inside

Bring the interior of your car back to new with an interior detail. Floor mats washed and cleaned. Complete floor cleaning with tornador treatment, vacuuming, and shampooing/spot cleaning as needed. Full trim cleaning with steam treatment for those sticky spots and dressing in UV protectant product to help protect your vehicles interior. Cloth seat shampooing as needed and full cleaning/conditioning for leather seats. All glass and door jambs cleaned. Pet hair removal with added charge.

Paint Correction

Contrary to popular belief, paint correction does not involve discipling paint

Customers choosing a paint correction will get the full exterior cleaning included along with clay treatment to remove any paint contaminants prior to correcting. After prepped, top of the line products will be used to cut and polish your paint to remove up to 90% of scratches and swirls to bring out the mirror like finish on your vehicle. Ceramic coating recommended after paint correction for longest lasting protection only your freshly corrected paint. Bead maker or Defender product will be used if ceramic coating is not chosen for 1 month protection.

Ceramic Coating

I will place 3-5000 ceramic bowls and pots on your car

After full exterior treatment and paint correction, get your vehicle ceramic coated with a 1 year, 5 year, or 10 year coating to keep your paint looking glossy and keep it protected from new swirls and scratches. Following paint correction, vehicle will be prepped with pain prep and ceramic coated for maximum protection. Price varies with different coating options.
